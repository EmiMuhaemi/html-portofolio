The Best Underatted Anime Accourding to Envy

My top 3 underatted anime of all-time.

    Noragami

    The story is about God Yato, a minor deity who dreams of having millions of followers. His daily life consists of odd jobs, all for a payment of 5 Yen.

    Death Parade

    The story is set after death, where there is no heaven or hell, only a bar that stands between reincarnation and oblivion.

    Quanzhi Gaoshou

    The story is about Ye Xiu, nicknamed the "Battle God" due to his skills and contributions to the game over the years. However, he is forced to retire from the team and leave his gaming career behind.
